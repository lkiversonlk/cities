About Reproduce

User can reproduce new tokenes base on the token they owned. A new CitiesToken will be randomly created around the origin token. The reproduce cost will be the 60% of the Origin token's price.If the place around the token is all taken. This token will be marked Unreproducible.

Token's Level

About Citiestoken's Level

1.There is 3 level Citiestoken:

  • Level-L: Large token，[about 1,000,000 ㎡ in City, and cover 100 Level-M tokens]
  • Level-M: Middle token，[about 10,000 ㎡ in City, and cover 100 Level-S tokens]
  • Level-S: Small token， [about 100 ㎡ in City, and cover none tokens]

    Earn Trading Commission

    The Owner of higher level token can earn 5% commission from Each transaction of the lower level token which covered.

    For example, the owner of the Level-M token {10.111,12.111} can earn 5% commission on each transaction of the 100 tokens: {10.1110,12.1110}~{10.1119,12.1119}, and no times limited.

    So, The owner of this Citiestoken just like owned a small Stock Exchange: The more flourishing the transactions, the more revenue.
